## 10 Gigabit Ethernet (IEEE 802.3ae Working Group, 10Gbps Ethernet)

- 10Gbps
- compatible with standard, fast, gigabit ethernet
- 48-bit address
- same frame format
- LAN말고도 MAN, WAN에서도 사용 가능
